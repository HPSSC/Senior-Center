Hattie Palmer Staton Senior Center of Excellence




The Hattie Palmer Staton Senior Center of Excellence,
located in Scotland Neck, NC, offers a variety of classes
and activities for older adults, including fitness classes, games,
Bible Study, and more. They have a fitness center for
adults age 60 and above, a walking trail for everyone, and
such sports as cornhole, shuffleboard, Billiards, and horseshoes.

The mission of the Center is to provide well rounded programs
and services for older adults in the community. This is done through
social activities, health and nutrition resources, and providing
a meaningful quality of life in a safe environment.
